Material 3 Tooltip — shows a label (plain) or a titled explanation (rich) on hover/focus.

```jsx
<Tooltip text="즐겨찾기"><IconButton icon="star" /></Tooltip>
<Tooltip rich subhead="새 기능" text="이제 폴더를 공유할 수 있습니다." position="bottom">
  <IconButton icon="info" />
</Tooltip>
```

Props: `text`, `rich`, `subhead`, `position` (`top`/`bottom`/`left`/`right`), `children` (anchor).
